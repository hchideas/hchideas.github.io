大胆买入，大多数时间持币，谨慎卖出

更新于: 2018-04-13 | 分类于 洞见 | | 热度: ℃
神童说：2018 年，币圏需要胆大心细的你！


        

        

Those waiting to cash out at the bottom price may feel at loss.



准备抄底的人还有一大把，心里可能很失落。



However, you probably should take a look at this story.



你应该听听这个故事：



In January 2017, Amanda grabbed a 10CNY virtual red pocket from a digital tokens speculation group, then she changed that money into XRP (Ripple) at 0.02CNY/token, and received 200CNY after she sold them out at 0.4CNY. After that she took a purchase of NEM (New Economy Movement) and sold out at 0.9CNY, and received 4,000CNY out of it. She then brought in LTC (Litecoin) at 27CNY/token while getting 26,000CNY when she sold out at 180CNY. Before DOGE’s price plummeted, she sold out at 0.029 and got 520,000CNY. She spent them all on Bit-shares at 0.25 while profited 5,200,000CNY when sold at 3CNY. Followed by purchasing ETH (Ethereum) at 300 and her fortune rose to 30,000,000CNY after she sold at 2,300. She bravely invested all on ETC (Ethereum classic) before its price fell to almost 100CNY, and sold out at 400CNY, which made it effortless for her to reach the goal of earning 100,000,000CNY.



2017年1月她在一个炒币群抢到10元的红包！用抢的红包在0.02的时候买入了瑞波币，0.4的时候抛的，她一下有了200元！然后她买了新经币0.9抛的，她就有了4000元，接着她在莱特币27的时候买入，180抛的，这样她就有了26000元，接着她又全仓了狗狗币，0.029抛的，她有了52万元，之后成功逃顶。在比特股跌到0.25的时候全仓，3元抛的，她就有了520万，她又在300元的时候买了以太坊，2300元抛的，然后她身价到了3000万。之后在即将跌到100元的ETC全仓杀入，在400元的时候卖了，赚它一个亿的小目标就轻松完成了！



Everyone wishes to buy in and sell out at the perfect time just like her, however, the probability of making profits with the least amount of money is extremely low.



每个人都想次次神操作，用最少的资金博取最大的利益，但这个概率低到爆。



Those who prefer margin trading might find themselves at loss now. Never am I fond of it unless I know with absolute certainty, otherwise I will not take the risk. That is because I have seen many who have already suffered from it, winning the bet the first nine times and lost all in the tenth game thus brought an end to oneself.



看见很多人喜欢玩高抛低吸，现在是不是正独自抹泪。我一直都不喜欢玩高抛低吸，除非有十足的把握，否则不会去做这样的事情，原因是在上面吃过太多亏，高抛低吸搞对了九次，最后一次搞错了，就玩完了。



A quick question for the new investors who favor margin trading, suppose that you sell out at 250CNY, then the market fell to 251 after a surge in price. What is your next move? What if you don’t purchase and lose the opportunity to buy in at this price ever again? What if the price becomes lower after you choose to buy in, and what would you feel about it?



对玩高抛低吸的嫩韭菜，我想问你250抛出去，然后币价涨了一波，接着跌到251，你买还是不买？不买要是再也买不到了怎么办，买了的话又觉得买亏了怎么办？



You may find it almost impossible to answer this question in view of a changing market. The right thing to do when facing such an opportunity with huge potential, simply is to buy in, particularly when the price collapse. Most of the mass are incapable of mastering margin trading, also keep this in mind, do not waste a chance over just a few bucks, since in the long run, none of these matters. On the other hand, should the price slump, do not attempt to get in all at a time. It is suggested to build part of your position as no one knows for sure whether there is a even lower price for you to build position.



面对瞬息万变的市场，是不是很难回答上面的问题？其实，看好的东西，只管买就好，特别是暴跌的时候，就更加不要犹豫了，不要玩什么高抛低吸，绝大多数人没这个能力，也不要为几块钱的蝇头小利错失良机，长远来看，这都不是事儿。另外，暴跌的时候，也不要重仓，建一部分仓位即可，谁也不知道有没有更低的底等着你建仓。



When the time comes I do not have the money to build position, someone said to me. Truth be told, if you spend 2,000CNY on the major cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin on a monthly basis, you probably will wait until forever to actually make some money out of it. For those with less capital in hand, there is another way for you to play, and that is to fight your way into the market and try to make as much seed money as you possibly can. Given that the amount of principal is low, you can always finance some more in case of a hold up. In the cryptocurrency world, it all comes down to the information you could receive and the analysis of it. Therefore exert your best efforts to seek and select the information, then make the best use of it to make some breakthroughs. Do not be greedy.



有人跟我说，很多机会来临，没钱建仓，其实吧，要我说你这每个月投2000块钱的比特币等主流币种，投到猴年马月也赚不了几个钱。本金少也有本金少的玩法，那就是拿着这些本金去市场搏杀积累点原始资金，由于本来就没多少钱，万一被套了就当没了，筹措点资金再战就好了。币圈说到底还是受消息面影响较大，所以你就得苦点累点，眼观六路耳听八方，打游击战定点突破，见好就收。



Suppose you do are not able to do so and lack the perseverance, it’s best for you to just get some pocket money buying the popular currencies. Earnings out of this would far exceed savings from bank deposits.



如果你连这个能力和魄力都不具备，那么老老实实买买主流币种挣点零花钱也不错，比存在银行强不少。



 Currency trading is all about buying and selling, and these two words reveals your true personality. To trade currency to is to reap from others’ cognitive bias. The year of 2018 will witness the outburst in this regard, so better equip yourself with the necessary skills to truly grasp this unprecedented and once in a life opportunity. At the same time, benefit from the fluctuating cryptocurrency world.  Speaking of which, I have to advise the book from ours, once you read it you will realize that, you were not able to gain from others’ cognitive bias only because your have not read RenZhiTuWei-from Cai Leilei. 



炒币无非就是买卖，但买卖二字能折射出你是什么样的人。炒币也是你在收别人的认知税，2018年会是爆发的一年，所以赶紧提高自身的境界会让你在跌宕起伏的币圈行走的更加游刃有余。说到这，忍不住给自家的书打个广告——可能你没收到别人的认知税，是因为你的生命中少了《认知突围》_蔡垒磊
