Those waiting to cash out at the bottom price may feel at loss.


However, you probably should take a look at this story.


In January 2017, Amanda grabbed a 10CNY virtual red pocket from a digital tokens speculation group, then she changed that money into XRP (Ripple) at 0.02CNY/token, and received 200CNY after she sold them out at 0.4CNY. After that she took a purchase of NEM (New Economy Movement) and sold out at 0.9CNY, and received 4,000CNY out of it. She then brought in LTC (Litecoin) at 27CNY/token while getting 26,000CNY when she sold out at 180CNY. Before DOGE‚Äôs price plummeted, she sold out at 0.029 and got 520,000CNY. She spent them all on Bit-shares at 0.25 while profited 5,200,000CNY when sold at 3CNY. Followed by purchasing ETH (Ethereum) at 300 and her fortune rose to 30,000,000CNY after she sold at 2,300. She bravely invested all on ETC (Ethereum classic) before its price fell to almost 100CNY, and sold out at 400CNY, which made it effortless for her to reach the goal of earning 100,000,000CNY.


Everyone wishes to buy in and sell out at the perfect time just like her, however, the probability of making profits with the least amount of money is extremely low.


Those who prefer margin trading might find themselves at loss now. Never am I fond of it unless I know with absolute certainty, otherwise I will not take the risk. That is because I have seen many who have already suffered from it, winning the bet the first nine times and lost all in the tenth game thus brought an end to oneself.


A quick question for the new investors who favor margin trading, suppose that you sell out at 250CNY, then the market fell to 251 after a surge in price. What is your next move? What if you don‚Äôt purchase and lose the opportunity to buy in at this price ever again? What if the price becomes lower after you choose to buy in, and what would you feel about it?


You may find it almost impossible to answer this question in view of a changing market. The right thing to do when facing such an opportunity with huge potential, simply is to buy in, particularly when the price collapse. Most of the mass are incapable of mastering margin trading, also keep this in mind, do not waste a chance over just a few bucks, since in the long run, none of these matters. On the other hand, should the price slump, do not attempt to get in all at a time. It is suggested to build part of your position as no one knows for sure whether there is a even lower price for you to build position.


When the time comes I do not have the money to build position, someone said to me. Truth be told, if you spend 2,000CNY on the major cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin on a monthly basis, you probably will wait until forever to actually make some money out of it. For those with less capital in hand, there is another way for you to play, and that is to fight your way into the market and try to make as much seed money as you possibly can. Given that the amount of principal is low, you can always finance some more in case of a hold up. In the cryptocurrency world, it all comes down to the information you could receive and the analysis of it. Therefore exert your best efforts to seek and select the information, then make the best use of it to make some breakthroughs. Do not be greedy.


Suppose you do are not able to do so and lack the perseverance, it‚Äôs best for you to just get some pocket money buying the popular currencies. Earnings out of this would far exceed savings from bank deposits.


Currency trading is all about buying and selling, and these two words reveals your true personality. To trade currency to is to reap from others‚Äô cognitive bias. The year of 2018 will witness the outburst in this regard, so better equip yourself with the necessary skills to truly grasp this unprecedented and once in a life opportunity. At the same time, benefit from the fluctuating cryptocurrency world.
